The Strategic Premise

Right idea. Right founder.
A thirty-family runway.

VitaKavana has the vision, the category, and the founder. What this plan addresses is the system underneath the brand: how prospects discover it without dilution, how conversations turn into commitments, and how Jordan's relational capital becomes a repeatable engine.

The work is not more marketing. It is commercial infrastructure that operates below the waterline — invisible to members, indispensable to growth.

The Premise 02 / 06
Brand Strategy · 4 Moves

Brand as infrastructure.

The symbolic architecture that makes a UHNW patriarch say "these people understand how we think."

B—01

Operationalize the Category

Add "The Architecture" + "What a Year Looks Like" pages. Define the language: anticipatory care, family protocol, health architecture. Categories belong to whoever defines them.

B—02

Case Narratives, Not Studies

Three archetypal narratives — HIPAA-compliant, ungated by request. The Founder Who Deferred His Health, The Multigenerational Risk, The Founder's Wife.

B—03

Disciplined Thought Leadership

4 LinkedIn posts/month from Jordan + quarterly private newsletter ("The Health Ledger") + 1 op-ed per quarter (WSJ, Barron's). Restraint is the position.

B—04

Material Identity

Welcome Kit (linen case, Mohawk paper). Bespoke proposals with the family's name on the cover. Quarterly reports delivered by courier. Materiality signals seriousness.

Brand Strategy 03 / 06
Commercial Strategy · 5 Moves

Sales as a relational system.

UHNW prospects don't move through funnels. They move through introductions, rituals, and waiting.

S—01

YPO Mosaic Leverage

Jordan is already founding member + Global Impact Officer. Chapter-level Health Forums, not external speaking. 3-5 closes from this channel alone.

S—02

Private Forum Series

5 curated Forums in Y1 — Miami, NYC, Aspen, Dallas, Palm Beach. 8-10 guests, peer learning format, off-the-record. 1-2 families per Forum.

S—03

MFO Alliances

Tier-1 Multi-Family Offices. No fee referrals (violates fiduciary duty). The currency is status, access, and relational credit. Annual Advisor Council in Aspen.

S—04

Premium Discovery Process

"Request an Introduction" form. 45-min Discovery with Jordan. Family Health Brief delivered in 5 days. Bespoke Proposal. The 7-day pause.

S—05

UHNW Ecosystems

Tiger 21, Milken, Aspen Ideas, R360. Speaker not sponsor. Editorial in Robb Report, Town & Country. Contributors become category authorities.
